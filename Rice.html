Traditional Fried Rice

Traditional Fried Rice is a dish that originated from the southern part of Nigeria, it is known to be very spicy

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of rice.
  • 4 cups of sliced Cabbage.
  • 2 cups of sliced Carrot.
  • 2 cups of sliced Green beans.
  • Half cup of peas.
  • 1 cup of sliced Green pepper.
  • Cow Liver {300g}.
  • 2kg of Chicken.
  • 200ml Vegetable oil.
  • 1 cup sweet corn.
  • 1 cup of sliced Onions.
  • Curry powder (coloring spice) {2 Tablespoons}.
  • 1 teaspoon ground pepper
  • Salt to taste.

steps

  1. Step 1 Wash and precook the chicken and liver together using a teaspoon of salt, half cup of sliced onions, a teaspoon of thyme spice, a teaspoon of curry, two seasoning cubes teaspoon each of powdered ginger and garlic). Allow the meat to cook for 15 minutes then pick to grill or deep fry. Remember to set the chicken stock (meat water) aside. I serve fried rice with either fried or grilled meat.
  2. Step 2 Chop the cabbage, green beans, carrot, liver, green peppers and set aside. The sample image below shows you some ingredients in small quantities.
  3. Step 3 Parboil the 4 cups of rice also and cook with half of the meat stock (water from the meat) and two cups of water, more water if your rice is hard to cook. You want it almost done., about 70% done but not completely soft.
  4. Step 4 Set your cooking pot on heat and add 200ml of vegetable oil. You will fry some ingredients for fried rice.
  5. Step 5 Allow heating then add the sliced onions stir for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Step 6 Then add the chopped carrot and green beans, any of these two can actually go first.
  7. Step 7 Add chicken stock (meat water), ground pepper (optional), a teaspoon of salt and a seasoning cube then stir and taste, you may add more salt. Add the green peas. Stir for 1-2 minutes then add cabbage and the curry powder for yellowish fried rice. Taste for salt, it should taste spicy and look yellowish. If it tastes nice and colorful, then add the green pepper. Stir.
  8. Combine with fried or grilled chicken and serve.
